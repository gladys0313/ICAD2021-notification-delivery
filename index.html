Musical Feature Modification for Less Intrusive Delivery of Popular Notification Sounds

We present a novel method to deliver artificial notification sounds that have been commonly used in digital devices and for popular applications in a less intrusive but still adequately noticable manner.

More specifically, we first transfer the musical timbre of a notification sound into the timbre of the background music that a user is listening to, while preserving the original notification melody envelope to still associate the notification with its interpretation. Next, we harmoniously embed the timbre-transformed notification into the background music for delivery. In order to achieve a seamless integration, we adjust the amplitude, tempo, and the overall octave level of the notification to match these features of the music, and we embed the notification with fade-in and fade-out effects, while the embedding spot is selected by algorithmically checking and finding the segment of the music that shares the most similar musical features with the notification sound.

We collected 81 popular notification sounds from iOS/Android system and applications like Skype, please refer to our notification sound dataset).
